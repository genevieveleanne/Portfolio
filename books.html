My Books

The Moonlight Thrills Series

A collection of paranormal romance short stories set in a world where magical species (werewolves, vampires, fairies, wizards, etc.) publicly coexist with humans. Each book can be read as a standalone, but it's best to read them in order because some characters make an appearance in other stories.

Meryle and Lancelot's Adventures

A short story series that reimagines the Arthurian legends with Merlin's cousin, Meryle Evanders, as the main protagonist. Join her and Lancelot du Lac as they undertake magical quests, fall in love, and discover Merlin's plan for Camelot.
